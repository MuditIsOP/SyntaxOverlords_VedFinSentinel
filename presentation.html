Problem & Solution

🔴 The Problem

131B+ UPI transactions in FY2024 — massive attack surface
₹14,000 Cr lost to digital financial fraud annually
4–6 hours average detection lag with rule-based systems
15–30% false positive rate disrupts legitimate users
~95% flagged transactions have zero explainability
❌ No per-user behavioral baseline — same threshold for everyone
❌ Black-box ML models rejected by RBI compliance auditors

✅ Our Solution — VedFin Sentinel

1
9-Dimensional Behavioral Fingerprinting — ADI, GRI, DTS, TRC, MRS, BFI, BDS, VRI, SGAS per user per transaction
2
Hybrid ML Ensemble — XGBoost (known fraud) + Isolation Forest (zero-day novel attacks)
3
Vedic Mathematics Layer — Anurupyena checksum pre-filter + Nikhilam threshold calibrator
4
SHAP Explainability — Human-readable reason for every flagged transaction, RBI-compliant
5
Attack Simulation Lab — 3 live attack scenarios detected & blocked in real time
6
< 340ms Detection — vs industry average of 4–6 hours
